Projects

Sustainable Waste Management for Metals in Australia

Led a comprehensive research project focusing on developing innovative and sustainable waste management solutions for metal recycling in Australia. This project involved:

  • Conducting an extensive literature review on current metal recycling practices
  • Analyzing the environmental impact of existing methods
  • Proposing novel techniques to improve recycling efficiency and reduce waste
  • Collaborating with industry experts to ensure practical applicability of solutions

The project resulted in a detailed report that was well-received by faculty and is being considered for further development by a local recycling company.

Robotic Knee Clamp for Medical Procedures

Designed and developed a cutting-edge robotic knee clamp system aimed at improving precision in orthopedic surgeries. Key aspects of this project included:

  • Utilizing advanced CAD software for precise 3D modeling
  • Conducting finite element analysis to ensure structural integrity
  • Collaborating with a multidisciplinary team including medical professionals
  • Developing a functional prototype and conducting rigorous testing

This project not only enhanced my technical skills but also gave me valuable experience in medical device design and interdisciplinary collaboration.
